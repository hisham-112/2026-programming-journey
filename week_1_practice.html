What is Bouldering

  1. Bouldering is a form of rock climbing that is performed on small rock formations or artificial rock walls without the use of ropes or harness
  2. Unlike traditional climbing,bouldering focuses on short but intensive routes called "problems,"typically keeping climbers within a safe distance from the ground while they navigate challenging sequences of moves.
  3. Experience the perfect blend of phisical challenge and mental puzzle-solving as you navigate routes close to the ground,with nothing but a crash pad and determination.

Why Bouldering?

  • Whether youn are a complete beginner or an experienced athlete. climbers of all ages can challenge themselves,celebrate each other's successes,and build lasting friendships.
