How to get started

Unlike many outdoor sports, geting started with bouldering is surprisingly simple.All you need are some climbing shoes, chalk, and a positive attitude-most gyms even rent the equipment for you sessions.No prior experience or complex gear requrired!

The simple steps to getting started

  1. Find a bouldering gym near you.
  2. Rent or invent in a pair of climbing shoes.
  3. Learn basic bouldering techniques.
  4. start with with easy routes and gradually increase with difficulty.
  5. Join a bouldering community or club.
